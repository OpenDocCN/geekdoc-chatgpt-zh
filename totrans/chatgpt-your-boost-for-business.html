What is Chat-GPT and what is a prompt?

Before we get into the details, let's briefly clarify what Chat-GPT actually is and what is meant by a "prompt". Chat-GPT is an advanced artificial intelligence application based on the so-called "GPT-4" model. GPT stands for "Generative Pre-trained Transformer" – a language model that has been trained to understand and generate human-like texts. It can respond to a wide range of questions, write texts, suggest ideas, and even perform complex analysis. Chat-GPT's strength lies in its versatility and adaptability to different topics and industries.

A "prompt" in this context is a prompt that you give Chat-GPT to get a response or a specific type of content. A well-worded prompt is critical to how useful the generated response is. The prompts in this book are specifically designed to provide you with concrete and actionable solutions to common business challenges. They are designed to allow you to ask specific questions to Chat-GPT for directly actionable information or advice.
