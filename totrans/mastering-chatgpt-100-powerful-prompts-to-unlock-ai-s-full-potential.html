Introduction: Embracing AI in Everyday Life

Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming how we interact with the world, making complex tasks simple and creativity boundless. Among the myriad of AI tools available, ChatGPT stands out as a versatile assistant for professionals, students, and creators alike. However, to unlock its true potential, crafting the right prompts is crucial.

This book offers 100 actionable prompts across 12 categories to help you maximize ChatGPT's power. Each prompt is accompanied by examples of the type of responses you can expect, ensuring you can apply these tools effectively in real-life scenarios.

From organizing your day to mastering storytelling or even crafting professional emails, this guide is your step-by-step manual to become a ChatGPT power user.

Section 1: Productivity Prompts

Prompt:

17. Math Tutor

Prompt:

"Solve this problem step-by-step: A car travels 60 miles in 2 hours. What is its average speed?"

Example ChatGPT Response:

*"To calculate the average speed, divide the total distance by the total time:

Distance = 60 miles

Time = 2 hours

Average speed = Distance ÷ Time = 60 ÷ 2 = 30 miles per hour

So, the car’s average speed is 30 mph."*
